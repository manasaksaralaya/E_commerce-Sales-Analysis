# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends. The dashboard enables businesses to monitor key metrics and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and filters.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting

## Dataset
The dataset contains e-commerce transaction records, including:
- Order Details
- Customer Information
- Product Categories
- Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Discount
- Regional and State-wise Sales

## Project Workflow
- Data cleaning and preparation
- KPI calculation
- Pivot Table analysis
- Interactive dashboard creation
- Business insights and recommendations

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, Quantity, Profit Margin)
- Monthly Sales vs Profit Trend
- Category-wise Sales Distribution
- Profit Contribution by Category
- State-wise Sales Map
- Top 5 Customers
- Top 5 Products
- Interactive Slicers and Filters

## Key Insights
- Technology contributes the highest profit among all categories.
- Sales are distributed across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology, with Technology leading overall performance.
- California and New York generate the highest sales.
- Top customers contribute a significant share of total revenue.
- Sales and profit show seasonal peaks during certain months.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions and customers.
- Increase investment in high-profit product categories.
- Improve performance of underperforming categories.
- Use seasonal trends to optimize inventory and promotional campaigns.
- Expand customer-focused strategies to increase repeat purchases.
